Cyber Empower is a week-long beginner Python programming camp aimed to spread the passion for coding among middle school students. Cyber Empower works is hosted by TIBCO Software and is funded by the City of Palo Alto. More information can be found in the About Tab. Cyber Empower locations in Denver, Palo Alto, and San Francisco! Request for Cyber Empower to be held at your local TIBCO using the contact tab above.
